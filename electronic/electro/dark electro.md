# Dark Electro

## Foundational blueprint and early canon (1992)

**Placebo Effect — Mystress (Galleries of Pain, 1992)**
A primary-statement early dark electro cut: stark sequencing and claustrophobic atmosphere foreground mood and sound design rather than "rock" hybridity. It's emblematic of the early-90s Central European template where dread, sample-like texture, and vocal treatment become structural elements.

**yelworC — Blood In Face (Brainstorming, 1992)**
yelworC are repeatedly invoked in histories of "dark electro" as a named/recognized current, and this era captures the genre's cold ritualism and processed aggression. The track is a clean illustration of how electro-industrial mechanics can be re-aimed toward horror affect without turning into noise or techno-hardcore.
